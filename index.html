// Biography

About

Dr. Alka Jain is a distinguished Associate Professor of Economics at the prestigious Prime Minister College of Excellence (PMCoE), Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Government Arts & Commerce College, Indore — one of the premier government educational institutions in Madhya Pradesh. With over 32 years of dedicated teaching and research experience since beginning her academic career on 7 July 1994 in the Department of Higher Education, Madhya Pradesh, she has made outstanding contributions to the field of Economics through research, pedagogy, and institutional service.

Dr. Jain is a prolific scholar with more than 100 research publications to her credit, spanning peer-reviewed journals, UGC-listed journals, edited book volumes, national and international conference proceedings, and seminar presentations. Her research covers a wide spectrum — from macroeconomic policy and India's foreign trade to tribal development, MSME sector analysis, skill development, value-based education, and the enduring legacy of India's knowledge tradition (Bhartiya Gyan Parampara).

She earned her M.Phil. from Vikram University, Ujjain (1991) and her Ph.D. in Economics from Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV), Indore in 2016. She was promoted to Associate Professor through the UGC Career Advancement Scheme (CAS) in August 2015 and is a recognized Research Supervisor under DAVV. She has completed orientation and refresher courses at DAVV (Indore) and JNU (New Delhi). Throughout her career, she has been actively engaged in conference organization, paper presentation, question paper setting, institutional governance, and extension activities that strengthen the academic ecosystem.

// Research

Research Expertise

Indian Foreign Trade & Policy

India's evolving trade landscape post-1991 liberalization — export promotion, exchange rate volatility, rupee dynamics, trade barriers, and India's positioning in the global economy.

Macroeconomics & Development

Growth patterns, FDI inflows, human development index, labour migration, gender analysis of employment trends, and inclusive economic development strategies.

MSME Sector & Entrepreneurship

Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises in the globalization era — performance, employment generation, export prospects, and challenges facing Indian MSMEs.

Skill Development & HRD

Skill formation, vocational education, human resource development, synergy between skill formation and entrepreneurial development, and NEP 2020 implementation.

Value-Based Education

Communication approaches for holistic student development — integrating traditional Indian values with modern digital pedagogy in the Indian education system.

Bhartiya Gyan Parampara

India's ancient knowledge tradition — Vedanta, Ayurveda, Yoga, Sankhya philosophy — and their relevance to contemporary education and the National Education Policy 2020.

Tribal & Rural Economics

Tribal health and healthcare, agricultural development in tribal districts, poverty dynamics, and sustainable growth in underserved regions of Madhya Pradesh.

Infrastructure & Regional Economics

Infrastructure-growth nexus, inter-district educational disparities, sustainable growth in Madhya Pradesh, and tourism economics.

COVID-19 & Economic Crisis

Impact of COVID-19 pandemic on global and Indian economy — GDP contraction, employment loss, supply chain disruptions, sectoral analysis, and policy responses for economic recovery.
